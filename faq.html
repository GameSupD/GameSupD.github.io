Frequently Asked Questions

Why can I not play games?

If you get a .zip file when you download the game, you'll have to extract all the files in order to play. Your device might give you some alerts saying that the files are dangerous, but don't worry, they are not.

Can I contact GameSup?

Yes! You can go to this page to contact SuperGames-, the creator of GameSup.

How do I get my game in GameSup Dev?

You can contribute to GameSup Dev by filling in this form and sending it to supergames.contactme@gmail.com to get a page for your game in GameSup Dev!

Please note that your game might not get accepted in GameSup Dev and if it does, it may take some time for a page to be created.

Can I see the source code for the games?

Yes, you can! With GameSup Codes. Just keep in mind that not it is not available for every game.
