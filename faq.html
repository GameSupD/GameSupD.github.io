Frequently Asked Questions

Why can I not play games?

If you get a .zip file when you download the game, you'll have to extract all the files in order to play. Your device might give you some alerts saying that the files are dangerous, but don't worry, they are not. Also note that they are only made for Windows.

Can I contact GameSup?

Yes! You can go to this page to contact SuperGames-, the creator of GameSup.

Can I see the source code for the games?

No, you can no longer access the games' source code.
