Privacy Policy

By creating an account, none of your data will be visible to anyone, not even to us. It is stored only on your device and can only be used on other devices using the Account Transfer page.


When you contact GameSup by email, the email address you use will be visible to us. However, it is not shared to anyone and will only be used as a way you can get a reply for your email.


Your personal or non-personal information will not be collected by using the GameSup website or downloading games.


If you submit a game to GameSup Dev, only the information you provide in the form will be visible to others in the website. All games are reviewed before getting added, so if you share any information that we consider personal in the game sharing form, we will not add to GameSup Dev.


By using the GameSup website, you agree to the privacy policy. If you do not agree, you should not use the website.
